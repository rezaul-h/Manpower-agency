About us

Our Mission

Our mission at We Can Work is to create a better world by connecting talented individuals with companies that need them. As a recruitment startup, we specialize in helping domestic companies in Korea overcome their challenges in hiring engineers by matching them with skilled professionals from around the world. Our vision is to become the global leader in recruitment by providing excellent service that exceeds customer expectations.

Get to know us

Looking for a job?

For those looking for a job, we offer customized job postings that take into account your work experience, skills, areas of interest, and contract terms. We also provide options for both in-person work in Korea and remote work in your home country, along with assistance with securing visas and employment agreements.

Create developer profile

Seeking for a developer?

For companies seeking talented developers, we introduce only verified engineers through local HR staff, offering a simple and fast recruitment process without the need for membership registration. We provide options for full-time, project-based contract and dispatch, as well as remote work contract conclusion.

Issuing visas through professional administrators

Our workflow is designed to be efficient and hassle-free, with a streamlined recruitment process that includes job registration, job/developer search, scheduling interviews, and administrative support such as drafting employment contracts and issuing visas through professional administrators.
